Making it actually happen

Every pre-tax dollar into the 401(k) only costs you about 78 cents of take-home at your bracket — the match on top is a guaranteed return no savings account can touch. At minimum, contribute enough to capture the full match at both jobs.

The child tax credit lowers the tax you owe, so check your W-4 — if you get a big refund every year, you're over-withholding and could have that cash in each paycheck instead.
